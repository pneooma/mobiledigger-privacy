# Privacy Policy for MobileDigger

**Effective Date**: August 30, 2025

## Introduction

MobileDigger ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

## Information We Collect

### No Personal Data Collection
MobileDigger is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you.

### Local Data Only
All data processed by MobileDigger remains on your device:
- Audio files you select for organization
- Metadata extracted from your audio files
- User preferences and settings
- Statistics about your usage (files processed, liked, rejected)

### No Network Communication
MobileDigger operates completely offline and does not:
- Connect to the internet
- Send data to external servers
- Share information with third parties
- Use analytics or tracking services

## How We Use Information

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no information to use beyond what you directly control on your device.

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
All data is stored locally on your device using:
- iOS File System for audio files
- UserDefaults for app settings and preferences
- Core Data for internal metadata management

### Security
- No data leaves your device
- No encryption keys or passwords are stored
- No cloud synchronization

## Third-Party Services

MobileDigger does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or external platforms.

## Children's Privacy

MobileDigger does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require personal information.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over all data on your device.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App Store.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- Email: privacy@mobiledigger.app
- Website: https://mobiledigger.app

## Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:
- Apple App Store Guidelines
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
